    SnakeERC.io is a multiplayer version of the classic snake game on the blockchain where players control a snake and try to eat pellets to grow while avoiding collisions with other snakes. The goal is to become the longest snake on the leaderboard!

    The future of “.io” gaming on the blockchain! Get ready for an exciting twist on the beloved snake game. Bringing the timeless fun of slithering and growing your snake combined with thrilling Wager/P2E action. With SnakeERC.io we are introducing a whole new dimension to the gaming world!
